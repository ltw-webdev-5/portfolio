Health Calculators that predict your life expectancy and other cool things. The goal of this project was to create one cohesive illustration style that is versatile to be used in every aspect it may need.

The main challenge was dealing with such a heavy subject matter: life expectancy and end of life. It was important to keep the mood light and encourage users to complete the assessments.
