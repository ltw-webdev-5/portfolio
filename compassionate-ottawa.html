{% pattern sections/project-text-section sentence1="The clients initially came seeking fresh eyes to help them upgrade their website in order to make it more appealing to younger audiences. My team and I concluded that in order to bring a younger, more serious audience their way, they needed to expand upon their branding—especially their logo. The clients were very happy with the colours that were already being used to represent their brand, so I made sure to brand around the colours they had in place." sentence2="When working on the logo I really wanted to focus on the aspect of life—but more specifically, the cycle of life is what drove my ideas. Therefore, I chose to incorporate leaves into the logo, because leaves are in an endless cycle of renewal—they grow, change colours, fall from the trees, and then the cycle repeats. The same thing happens with people, except we grow from babies, to toddlers, to adults, to elders." %}
