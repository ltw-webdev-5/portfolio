" sentence2="I incorporated the colours of his most recent album Kids See Ghosts into the illustration to give it more personality, while also trying to bring attention to the album. I wanted to incorporate his name into the poster, and I tried a lot of different styles of text. I settled on making the text look kind of grungy and sketch-like to match the rest of the composition. This piece works as a simple poster, but it can also work on plenty of other things such as phone cases, shirts, etc." %}
