{% pattern cards/card-image-right heading="The Brand" text="Since Canada has only recently legalized edibles, there aren’t any studies done for what kind of people buy edibles. Due to this, I designed the brand to apeal to the demographic for cannabis. Research shows that more then half of cannabis consumers are between the ages of 21-39 and more then half are male. I also wanted this edibles company to have a theme. Taking the demographic into consideration, I decided to make the campany space themed and name it Space Cadet Edibles." image="/images/demographic-cannabis.jpg"%}
{% pattern cards/card-image-left heading="The Logo" text="When thinking of ideas for Space Cadet Edibles logo, I was looking at images to inspired me when I cam accross a picture of a cannabis leaf. I realized the top part of the leaf was the same shape as the tip of a rocket. I immediately started sketching and coming up with different variations." image="/images/leaf-rocket.jpg"%}
