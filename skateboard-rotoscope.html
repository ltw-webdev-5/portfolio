Skateboard Rotoscope

Project Scope

This project was a practice in rotoscoping.

Design Process

The process here was simple. After finding video inspiration for the type of movement I wanted, I began drawing out the video in diferent frames. I chose to skip frames when drawing in the skateboarder, which created a choppy look to the video giving it a more urban, street art feel.

I wanted the video to remain simple so it is purely line work with no extra effects on top.
