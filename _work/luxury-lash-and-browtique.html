Luxury Lash & Browtique

Diagram Illustrations

Challenge

The client needed 6 illustrated diagrams that are clear and descriptive as well as fits the brand identity.

Background

Luxury Lash & Browtique is a women’s beauty boutique in Orléans that started in early 2019. Since then, the business has been very stable receiving many clients daily. They offer a variety of services like lash extensions, eyebrow microblading and more. In August, 2019, the founder of Luxury Lash & Browtique was preparing to start running lash extension training classes. They reached out in need of custom diagrams for their training manual. “We are proud to provide Accredited training to become a Lash Technician or upgrade your current skills, as well as provide Accreditation towards becoming a Vanity Pro (Microcurrent technology aka the workout for your face!).”
— Luxury Lash & Browtique

Final Products

Each diagram incorporates the brand colours and font, making them appear as a cohesive set of graphics. The application of the brand colours along with the layouts make it easy to highlight and identify the importances in every composition. The client was very satisfied with these results and was happy to include them in their manual to share with new lash tech learners.
