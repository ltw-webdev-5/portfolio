Hi! I'm Megan Smith.

I got my start in graphic design doing photo manipulation pieces in Photoshop for fun. I did that for about a year and a half before coming into the program and I always thought that's all I was oging to do. After having come into the program I fell in love with lots more areas of design. I was never the best at drawing or animating but I've gotten so much practive over the 3 years.

My specialties are:

Illustration

Illustrations are a great touch to your personal brand. They give that extra bit of personality that photographs do not.

Photo Manipulation

Mixing and matching contents of different photographs to create an ethereal compostion. What's better than that?

Branding

A well developed brand is important because it's the first thing that customers or consumers get to know about you.

Motion Graphics

Nothing makes illustrations look better than making them move and diving deeping into their story.

UI/UX Design

User Interface/User Experience

The way a website looks is important, but what's more important is making sure it works properly and well.

Digital Design

Digital design is exactly what it sounds like. Design that stays digital, like motion, web banners, web design, etc.
